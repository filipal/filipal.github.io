Darth Maul Kitbashing

The head sculpt and the textures are done by me, the rest of the model was kitbashed out of smaller models provided to us by a lecturer

Programs used:

Zbrush: Assembling the model and sculpting the head

Photoshop: adding more detail to the ref sheet and more textures

About the Project

This project involved creating a highly detailed model of Darth Maul by combining various smaller models provided by our lecturer. The head sculpt and textures were created from scratch using Zbrush, while additional details and textures were added in Photoshop. The goal was to achieve a realistic and dynamic representation of Darth Maul, capturing his menacing and iconic look. This project challenged my skills in both 3D modeling and texturing, and provided an opportunity to explore different techniques in digital sculpting and kitbashing.
