Concept Sketches

This project showcases two distinct scenes: the Steampunk Workstation and the Apocalyptic Death Scene. Each scene was meticulously crafted using a variety of software tools including Zbrush, Maya, Substance Painter, and Unity. The Steampunk Workstation features intricate details and textures, with wireframe models providing a glimpse into the modeling process. The Apocalyptic Death Scene, inspired by "The Last of Us," highlights the desolation and destruction of a post-apocalyptic world. The project aims to demonstrate advanced texturing and modeling skills, with all assets created and developed by the artist.
