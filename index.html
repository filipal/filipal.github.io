About Me

I'm Paula, a passionate third-year student in the Game Art and Design course.


As a versatile 3D and 2D artist, I specialize in a wide array of skills, including animation, programming, texturing, retopology, and 3D modeling.

My primary passion, however, lies in character design and concept art. I thrive on exploring new media and genres, constantly pushing the boundaries of creativity.

With a keen eye for detail and a strong commitment to my craft, I aim to bring unique and compelling characters to life.

Whether it's for games, films, or any other creative projects, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality work that captivates and engages audiences.

I'm always eager to collaborate on exciting projects and take on new challenges in the dynamic world of game art and design.
